Who We Are

Driven by Purpose, Guided by Values

Renso Foundation is built on a clear mission and bold vision to transform lives across Canada.

Our Mission

Empowering People, Strengthening Communities

Renso Foundation is a multicultural non-profit organization dedicated to empowering individuals and strengthening communities across Canada through leadership development, economic opportunity, cultural connection, and compassionate healthcare initiatives. We believe every person deserves the tools and support to thrive.

Our Vision

A Nationally Recognized Force for Change

To become a nationally recognized foundation that bridges diverse communities, inspires civic leadership, and creates measurable social impact in Canada. We envision a country where cultural diversity is celebrated as our greatest strength, and every community has the resources to flourish and lead.

Our Story

Born from Community, Built for Community

Renso Foundation was founded in 2018 by a group of passionate community leaders who recognized a gap: Canada's multicultural communities had tremendous potential but limited resources and infrastructure to organize, lead, and uplift themselves.

What started as small neighbourhood gatherings and fundraising dinners grew into a structured non-profit with registered charitable status, a dedicated board of directors, and programs spanning healthcare, education, economic development, and cultural celebration.

Today, Renso Foundation serves communities across the Greater Toronto Area and beyond, with plans to expand programs nationally. Our work is driven by volunteers, sustained by donors, and measured by the real change we create in people's lives.
